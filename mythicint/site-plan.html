Overview

Purpose

This website provides information about the upcoming World of Warcraft: Dragonflight Mythic Dungeon International Tournament for 2023. The purpose is to try and draw E-Sport fans towards World of Warcraft to see the kind of different competition and gameplay that it offers.

Audience

The audience is people who enjoy computer games and MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game) style games.

Content

Home page

World of Warcraft is an MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game) which means that its completely online. You are constantly around and interacting with other players who are in your region, area, world, etc. Dungeons in the game are referred to as ‘instanced content’ where you will be put through a loading screen and put in an instance that has specific rules and restrictions. If I go into a Dungeon, I can only bring up to 4 other players with me, so five players being the max number.

Images for the Home page

Dungeons

Dungeons have levels of difficulty which bring us to the core of what Mythic Dungeon International is – Mythic+ (called Mythic Plus). Mythic+ goes beyond the regular difficulties of Normal, Heroic, and Mythic, wherein you have a Keystone that has a corresponding number to determine the level of difficulty and how many affixes are attached to that Keystone.

Images for Dungeons

Schedule

The Mythic Dungeon International (MDI) returns with global competitions for its 7th year, pitting the best Mythic Dungeon teams in a head-to-head race to the finish line. Eight teams from around the world will advance to the Dragonflight Season 1 Global Finals where they will race for their share of $300,000.
