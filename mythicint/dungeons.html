Dungeon Mythic+ Affixes

Dungeons have levels of difficulty which bring us to the core of what Mythic Dungeon International is – Mythic+ (called Mythic Plus). Mythic+ goes beyond the regular difficulties of Normal, Heroic, and Mythic, wherein you have a Keystone that has a corresponding number to determine the level of difficulty and how many affixes are attached to that Keystone.

For example, say I get a Keystone for Court of Stars Dungeon at +4 difficulty. That will give me 2 affixes and be considered 4 levels tougher than a plain Mythic Dungeon, 4 levels tougher than a Heroic Dungeon, and 6 levels tougher than a Normal Dungeon.

An affix is a modifier applied to a mythic keystone at set levels. Different combinations occur every week, with the combinations repeating on a set schedule.

Level 2 Affixes

Fortified – Non-bosses have more health and deal 30% more damage.

Tyrannical – Bosses have 30% more health and deal 15% more damage.

Level 4 Affixes

Bolstering – Non-boss enemies buff nearby allies on death, increasing maximum health by 15% and damage by 20%.

Bursting – Non-boss enemies explode on death, causing all players to suffer flag damage that scales with keystone level.

Raging – Non-boss enemies enrage at 30% health, dealing 75% increased damage until killed.

Sanguine – Non-boss enemies will leave a pool on the ground on death. This pool heals enemies and damages players for a percentage of their maximum health.

Spiteful – Spirits rise from the corpses of non-boss enemies and chase down random players, dealing heavy melee damage.

Level 7 Affixes

Explosive – Enemies have a chance to summon an Explosive Orb nearby that will explode after six seconds, inflicting damage 40% of the player’s maximum health as damage.

Grievous – While below 90% health, players are afflicted with Grievous Wound (ticking bleed damage).

Quaking – Players will periodically Quake, interrupting spell casts of the player, Nearby allies take 40% of their maximum health as damage.

Storming – Tornadoes spawn around enemy creatures while in combat.

Volcanic – Enemies cause eruptions of flame near distant players. Additional Volcanoes may appear near existing volcanoes.

Level 10/Seasonal Affix

Thundering – Enemies have 5% more health. While in combat, players are periodically overcharged with primal power – this power comes with great risk and failure to discharge it quickly can have stunning consequences.
