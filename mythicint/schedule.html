Mythic Dungeon International Schedule

The Mythic Dungeon International (MDI) returns with global competitions for its 7th year, pitting the best Mythic Dungeon teams in a head-to-head race to the finish line. Eight teams from around the world will advance to the Dragonflight Season 1 Global Finals where they will race for their share of $300,000.
